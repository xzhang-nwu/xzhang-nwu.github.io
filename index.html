About me

I am currently a lecturer in the School of Information Science and Technology at Northwest University, China. I received the PhD degree from Northwest University of China in 2022. My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, pattern recognition and Fine-grained image analysis.

    NWU-ABC-MRSI Dataset
    
          
    NWU-ABC-MRSI dataset contains 10242 multi-temporal remote sensing images of 3 object categories (i.e., vehicle, aeroplane, and ship). This dataset is publicly shared at https://github.com/XZNWU/NWU-ABC-MRSI/.
    
          
    Please cite the following paper when you use this dataset fully or partly:
          
  • Xiang Zhang, Hangzai Luo, Sheng Zhong, Lei Tang, Jinye Peng, Jianping Fan, "Movable Object Detection in Remote Sensing Images via Dynamic Automatic Learning," in IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, vol. 61, pp. 1-14, 2023, Art no. 2001314, doi: 10.1109/TGRS.2023.3270496.

    AIBD-Cars Dataset
    
            
    Identifying the detailed model of a car from an image is challenging for anyone but car experts, and collecting and labeling large-scale fine-grained data is daunting in general. We have constructed a fine-grained dataset named AIBD-Cars, which contains 28471 car images with complex backgrounds belonging to 196 fine-grained classes. This dataset is publicly available at https://github.com/XZNWU/AIBD-Cars.
    
            
    Please cite the following paper when you use this dataset fully or partly:
            
  • Xiang Zhang, Keran Zhang, Wanqing Zhao, Hangzai Luo, Sheng Zhong, Lei Tang, Jinye Peng, Jianping Fan, "A benchmark dataset and approach for fine-grained visual categorization in complex scenes," in Digital Signal Processing, 2023, 137: 104033.
